What to do 2022-12-28/P3D?

What

If 37C3 should not happen in 2023, we aim for a hacking, art, congress replacement, politics gathering and party from December 28th to December 30th 2023 in Leipzig with 500 attendees or more

This webseite will keep you up to date with whatever your winter holiday plans 2023 may be.

Why

In October 2022 it was finally announced that the Chaos Communication Congress 2022 will be cancelled for the third year in a row. For this period 2022 was called for decentralized events. In such a short term, it was not possible for hackers to plan events that total anything close to the 17.000 people that attended 36C3 in Messe Leipzig.

If Chaos Communication Congress is cancelled yet another year in succession, we don't want to be at home again. After three years of disappointment, we finally want to meet our co-hackers irl again. Drink some Tschunk, have a workshop, hear a talk, have a party, chill in the lounge, collect some stickers, touch some electronics, see an installation, paint a banner, find new friends, find old friends and collect some bottles!

How

At this point, nothing is settled but we have a location with a capacity of 500 people, possibly more when using additional premises within the building. This not your glossy Leipzig fair, this is a dirty yet full-featured underground venue. It has bars, it has music, it has lights. Toilets, chairs, light installations, lovely staff and in-house geeks. It has an outdoor area. It has an angel space and other behind-the-scenes infrastructure.

What it doesn't have is enough Sofas, enough Tables, showers, a sufficient kitchen and the rest it takes to feed all of us. It lacks workshop equipment, video streaming, DECT, enough internet, a sauna waggon, cocktail robots, ball baths and you.

Join in!

We are few individuals and we're in a very early stage of planning. We're looking for co-conspirators to finish up a concept, dissect tasks and find groups and individuals who take part in them.
